Welcome To Sandhiya’s Mehandi Designer

Welcome to the innovative world of henna art & design. Mehandi Creations offers a highly personalised and bespoke service to suit your individual needs and budgets.

Mehandi Creations are a new generation of artists, using unique techniques together with natural ingredients for the mehandi, to add depth to its colour for your special occasions to last for days to come.

We at Mehandi Creations, are available for all occasions such as weddings, mehandi parties, hen parties, fashion shows & corporate events to name a few.
